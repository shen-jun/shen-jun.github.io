Bio

I'm currently a first year Economics Ph.D. student in the School of Economics at The University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr Rafael Lopes de Melo. I'm especially interested in the research of Macroeconomics, Labor Economics, and Applied Econometrics. Previously, I graduated with a BSc (Honours) in Mathematics and Economics from the University of Toronto. Outside of research, I'm a professional ballet dancer and a data scientist.
